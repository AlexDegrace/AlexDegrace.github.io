Our Experts

James Power (Veterinarian)
I graduated in 2004 and joined the clinic in 2003. My favourite thing about the clinic is the way the staff works together as a team to provide fantastic care to our patients. What I love most about my job is helping with the well-being of our clients' animals. A fun fact about me is that I raise Morgan horses, have beef cattle, llamas, cats, and dogs. My first pet was Trixie, a pony.
Samuel Bilodeau (Registered Veterinary Technician)
From a young age, I always knew I wanted to work with animals. After graduating from Oulton College in 2010 I began working in a small veterinary practice in Sackville, NB. In 2013 I took a job working at an emergency and critical care veterinary hospital in Moncton, NB, then in 2017 I began working at MRAH. The best thing about MRAH is the staff, and how well we all work as a team, and how each team member treats each patient like their own family.
Marc Savoie (Associate Veterinarian)
I enjoy helping clients provide care for their pets. I graduated from the Atlantic Veterinary College on PEI in 2013. I joined the Mountain Road Animal Hospital team in May of 2016. The aspects of veterinary medicine that interest me the most are working senior pets, preventative care and dentistry. My first pet was a cairn terrier named Muffin. What I love most about my job is helping people provide the best care they can for their pets. My favourite thing about the clinic is that we have a very caring and passionate team. A fun fact about me is that I love the ocean!
Samira Saade (Registered Veterinary Technician)
I have always known that I wanted to work in veterinary medicine. I worked for a short while as an assistant in the Fredericton area but wanted to do more. I went to school at Oulton College for the veterinary technician program and graduated in 2014. I then moved to Newfoundland for 5 years, then in May 2019 moved back to New Brunswick and started here.
